Players Turn: Player One

  1. Choose who is Player One and Player Two.
  2. Player One goes first and is X on the board and Player Two goes second and is O on the board.
  3. Alternate your turns by clicking Xs and Os on the board until either player has three in a row, horizontally, vertically, or diagonally or until all squares on the grid are filled.
  4. If a player is able to draw three Xs or three Os in a row, then that player is the winner!
  5. If all squares are filled and neither player has made a complete row of Xs or Os, then the game is a draw!
